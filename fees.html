Chinese Herbal Medicine

Herbal prescription fees

Herbal medicine is offered alongside acupuncture or as a standalone service. Prescription costs vary depending on the complexity of your formula and the duration of the prescription.

Service Description Fee
Herbal consultation (standalone) Full case history, Chinese medicine diagnosis, and initial herbal prescription for patients not receiving acupuncture £55
Herbal prescription (alongside acupuncture) Personalised herbal formula prepared at or following your acupuncture appointment — no separate consultation fee From £18 / week
Herbal prescription review (standalone) Review and adjustment of your formula without an acupuncture session, for established patients £25
Granule / powder formulas Concentrated herbal granules (dissolved in water) — convenient, flexible dosing. Price varies by formula complexity From £18 / week
Patent pill formulas Pre-prepared classical Chinese formulas for simpler, acute presentations From £12

Herbal prescription costs are in addition to the acupuncture consultation fee and are priced separately, as the cost of your formula depends on the specific herbs required. Sue will always give you a clear indication of costs before prescribing.

Private health insurance

Balance Acupuncture accepts private health insurance claims from a number of major providers. Some insurers allow self-referral; others require a referral letter from your GP or a specialist consultant.

A receipt and our BAcC registration number are issued after every treatment for insurance purposes. Please confirm your cover with your insurer before booking.

For insurance enquiries, please call 07747 065296 or email info@balanceacupuncture.uk.

Cancellation policy

We kindly ask that you give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time to another patient.

Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the treatment cost.

We understand that circumstances sometimes change unexpectedly — please contact us as soon as possible and we will always try to be flexible where we can.

Common questions

Fees FAQ

How many treatments will I need?
This varies considerably depending on your condition, how long you have had it, and your individual response to treatment. After your first appointment, Sue will give you an estimated treatment plan. For acute conditions, a short course of 4–6 sessions may be sufficient. For long-standing chronic conditions, a longer commitment of weekly sessions is usually needed to achieve lasting change.
Are herbal medicine costs included in the acupuncture fee?
No — herbal prescriptions are costed separately, as the ingredients required vary considerably between patients. Your herbal formula cost will be explained clearly before any prescription is made. The weekly cost of a herbal prescription typically starts from around £18.
Can I claim my treatment costs back through my employer?
Many employers offer health cash plan benefits that include complementary therapies such as acupuncture. Check your employee benefits documentation or speak to your HR team. A receipt and VAT invoice can be issued for all treatments on request.
Do you offer a free initial consultation?
We offer a free 15-minute telephone call for anyone who would like to discuss their health concerns before committing to a full appointment. This is a relaxed, no-obligation conversation. Call 07747 065296 or use the contact form to request a call back.
