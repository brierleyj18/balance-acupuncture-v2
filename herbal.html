What is it?

Rooted in nature, tailored to you

Chinese herbal medicine can be used as a standalone treatment, or alongside acupuncture to help build on the work you and I do in clinic.

Each formula is chosen for you as an individual, looking at your symptoms, your overall health, and how things change over time. Unlike conventional medicines, which often focus on managing a specific symptom or diagnosis, Chinese herbal medicine takes a broader view — aiming to support the whole person and address root causes alongside the symptoms.

When professionally prescribed, Chinese herbal medicine offers a gentler, more personalised approach than conventional treatments, with formulas regularly adjusted to suit your needs and minimise unwanted effects wherever possible.

Chinese herbal medicine can be used alongside acupuncture to support your treatment between appointments and help build on the work we do in clinic.

The integrated approach

How herbal medicine supports acupuncture

Acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine are the two primary branches of Traditional Chinese Medicine — and they are designed to work in concert. Together, they offer a depth of therapeutic care that neither achieves alone.

Continuity

Acupuncture works in the session. Herbal medicine works between sessions — maintaining and extending the therapeutic momentum so your body continues to respond and heal around the clock.

Depth

Where acupuncture is particularly powerful for moving Qi and addressing structural imbalances, herbal medicine excels at nourishing, tonifying and addressing deep constitutional weaknesses that require sustained internal support.

Precision

Your herbal prescription is reviewed and refined at every appointment. As your health picture changes, your formula evolves with it — ensuring treatment is always precisely matched to where you are right now.

The process

Your herbal medicine journey

From your first conversation to your evolving prescription, here is how we approach herbal medicine at Balance Acupuncture.

Your consultation

I take a thorough case history, exploring not only your presenting symptoms but your overall constitution — digestion, sleep, energy, emotional state, and more. This holistic picture forms the foundation of your prescription.

Your diagnosis

Using the diagnostic principles of Chinese medicine — including tongue and pulse assessment — I identify your underlying pattern of disharmony. This is what your prescription is built around, not just your symptoms.

Your formula

Your personalised herbal formula is prepared using carefully sourced, quality-tested herbs. I will explain what each formula is designed to address and answer any questions you have about the ingredients or process.

Ongoing review

Your prescription is reassessed at every appointment and adjusted as your health evolves. Herbal medicine is dynamic — your formula should change as you do.

Conditions treated

What herbal medicine can help with

Chinese herbal medicine has a rich clinical history in treating a broad spectrum of health conditions. The following are areas in which I have particular experience and clinical confidence.

Women's Health & Hormonal Balance

Menstrual irregularities, PMS, PCOS, endometriosis, perimenopause, menopause, fertility support — Chinese herbal medicine has a particularly deep tradition in this area.

Digestive Health

IBS, bloating, reflux, constipation, diarrhoea, weak digestion, nausea — herbal formulas can gently and profoundly regulate and restore digestive function.

Stress, Anxiety & Emotional Wellbeing

Chronic stress, anxiety, low mood, emotional exhaustion — herbal medicine can nourish the nervous system and support a calmer, more resilient state of mind.

Sleep & Fatigue

Insomnia, disturbed sleep, chronic fatigue, low energy — herbal prescriptions work to address the root cause of depletion and support the body's natural restorative processes.

Skin Conditions

Eczema, psoriasis, acne, urticaria — Chinese herbal medicine has a long and well-documented tradition in dermatology, addressing skin conditions from the inside out.

Respiratory & Immune Health

Recurrent colds, hayfever, allergic rhinitis, asthma — herbal medicine can strengthen immune function and reduce hypersensitivity responses over time.

Pain & Inflammation

Chronic pain, arthritis, fibromyalgia, headaches — herbal formulas with anti-inflammatory and circulation-promoting properties can be profoundly effective for pain conditions.

Reproductive & Fertility Support

Male and female fertility, pregnancy preparation, hormonal optimisation — herbal medicine is frequently used alongside acupuncture to support reproductive health and conception.

Chinese herbal medicine doesn't treat your diagnosis — it treats you. Two people with the same condition may have entirely different prescriptions, because the aim is always to restore your individual balance. — Sue, Balance Acupuncture
Safety & quality

Professional oversight you can trust

Chinese herbal medicine, when prescribed by a qualified and registered practitioner, is a safe and rigorously practised form of natural medicine. I adhere to the highest standards of professional conduct and sourcing.

Q

Quality-tested herbs

All herbs are sourced from reputable suppliers with full quality testing and traceability. Only herbs free from contaminants and correctly identified are used in your prescription.

R

Qualified prescriber

I am a qualified Chinese herbal medicine practitioner. I take a full case history, including current medications, before prescribing and monitor your response throughout treatment.

E

Ethical & sustainable

We are committed to using herbs that are ethically sourced and sustainably harvested. No endangered species or products of animal origin are ever used.

Important note

Always inform me of any prescription medications or supplements you are taking before beginning herbal medicine. While Chinese herbs are natural, some can interact with pharmaceutical drugs and I will advise you accordingly. Your safety is always the first consideration.
